Connector audit + SP2013 workflow scan this week. See Action Required tab.

⚠️ Action Required / Deadlines 5 items
HIGH — DUE MARCH 31 Office 365 Connectors in Teams — Retire This Week
MSP AdminAll ClientsMC Retirement
O365 Connectors in Teams — the webhook-based integrations for third-party services — are retired March 31 as part of Microsoft's Secure Future Initiative. Existing connectors will stop delivering messages after this date.
Audit all Teams channels across client tenants for active O365 connectors. Migrate to the Workflows app in Teams immediately. Use PowerShell: Get-TeamChannel + connector APIs. This week only.
HIGH — DUE APRIL 2 SharePoint 2013 Workflows — Full Retirement
MSP AdminMC542767
SharePoint 2013 workflows will be fully retired for all tenants on April 2, 2026. Any remaining workflows stop functioning — no migration path is automatic.
Run PnP PowerShell Get-PnPWorkflowDefinition across all client SharePoint sites. Migrate any hits to Power Automate before April 2. 10 days remaining.
HIGH — DUE JUNE 1 Teams Phone Hardware Retirement (Poly, Yealink, Crestron)
MSP Admin + End UsersMC1229952
Specific Poly Trio, Yealink, and Crestron Teams Phone devices will completely lose the ability to sign in and make/receive calls on June 1. No software fix — physical replacement only.
Audit all Teams Phone device inventories across clients. Cross-reference against MC1229952 affected model list. Start procurement conversations for any affected devices now — hardware lead times can exceed 4–6 weeks.
HIGH — DUE MAY 15 Teams Web — ECMAScript 2022 Browser Enforcement
MSP Admin + End UsersMC1230888
Teams on the web will block users on non-ECMAScript 2022-compliant browsers starting May 15. Affected users will be fully locked out — not degraded. Desktop and mobile apps are unaffected.
Identify clients using Teams via browser (kiosks, thin clients, unmanaged devices). Push browser updates via Ninja. Redirect browser-dependent users to Teams desktop app now to build the habit before enforcement.
MEDIUM — DUE MARCH 31 Defender for Android — Android 10 Support Ends
End Users (mobile)Managed Devices
Microsoft Defender on Android 10 devices will no longer receive security updates or new features after March 31. The app continues to function but is effectively unprotected from new threats.
Identify Android 10 devices in Intune. Push OS upgrade prompts. Flag to clients with managed mobile devices. Include in March end-user comms.
🛡️ Security & Compliance 6 items
HIGH Defender for Office 365 — URL Click Alerts Now in Teams
MSP AdminRoadmap 557549Business Premium
Microsoft Defender now detects malicious link clicks inside Teams messages — not just email. Admins see alerts like "User clicked potentially malicious URL" in the Defender portal. Requires Defender for Office 365 Plan 1 (included in Business Premium).
Verify MDO Plan 1 alert policies include Teams URL click alerts in all Business Premium tenants. Check Defender portal → Alert policies and confirm Teams coverage is active.
HIGH DLP Expanded — Blocks Copilot from Processing Protected Files
MSP AdminMC1234661Copilot Clients
DLP policies now block Microsoft 365 Copilot from processing protected files across all storage locations, and DLP coverage extends to Copilot prompts and responses. Without updated policies, Copilot could surface sensitive data.
For any clients using or evaluating Copilot: review and update DLP policies to include Copilot scope in Purview. Critical governance step before Copilot expansion.
MEDIUM Teams Report a Suspicious Call
BothMC1223828Rollout: April 15–30
Users can flag suspicious one-to-one calls directly from Teams call history. Reports surface in the Microsoft Defender portal and Teams Admin Center. Helps detect phone-based social engineering (vishing).
Enable enhanced reporting in Defender portal ahead of April rollout. Add to Huntress SAT messaging — finance, HR, and helpdesk are prime vishing targets.
MEDIUM Conditional Access — Resource Exclusion Enforcement Tightened
MSP AdminEntra / CA Policies
Resource exclusions in Conditional Access policies will now be consistently enforced. Previously there were enforcement gaps that may have allowed unintended access paths through excluded resources.
Audit CA policies across all client tenants, especially any with resource exclusions. Test in report-only mode first. A silently non-enforced exclusion may now start blocking legitimate users.
MEDIUM Passkey Profiles in Microsoft Entra (Preview)
MSP AdminMC1221452March 2026
Entra now supports passkey profiles and synced passkeys in preview, enabling phishing-resistant passwordless authentication that goes beyond Microsoft Authenticator.
Begin passkey readiness evaluation for Business Premium clients. Align with existing Authenticator push campaigns. Passkeys are the next step in eliminating password-based risk.
MEDIUM SharePoint — Content Security Policy Now Enforced
MSP AdminCustom SP Clients
SharePoint now enforces Content Security Policy (CSP), controlling which scripts and resources pages can load. Significantly reduces XSS, code injection, and clickjacking risk — but may break custom web parts or embedded scripts.
Alert clients with custom SharePoint web parts or embedded third-party scripts. Test any non-standard SharePoint customizations in a test site immediately.
🤖 Copilot & AI 4 items
MEDIUM Copilot Chat in Shared & Delegate Mailboxes
End UsersRoadmap 554936March 2026
Copilot Chat is now available inside shared and delegate mailboxes in the new Outlook for web. Users with full access can use Copilot for summarization, drafting, and Q&A within the shared mailbox context. Auto-enabled — no admin action required.
Inform clients using shared mailboxes (reception, support@, info@) who have Copilot licenses. Proactive communication prevents confused helpdesk calls about the new AI interface.
MEDIUM AI Watermarking Policy for Copilot-Generated Content
MSP AdminMC1221451Opt-in
Admins can now enable a policy to add visual or audio watermarks to video and audio content generated or altered by AI in Microsoft 365. Must be explicitly enabled via Cloud Policy service. Images are out of scope for this release.
Evaluate for clients in regulated industries (healthcare, legal, financial services) with AI governance requirements. Enable via Cloud Policy service. Good QBR conversation starter on AI governance readiness.

👥 Teams & Collaboration 4 items
MEDIUM Teams Live Events — Retirement Path Active
BothMC1226495Full Retirement: Feb 28, 2027
Teams Live Events and associated Graph APIs will be fully retired by February 2027, with new Live Event scheduling restrictions beginning before the deadline. Microsoft's recommended replacement is Teams Town Halls, which offers comparable large-scale broadcast capability.
Identify clients using Live Events for all-hands, board meetings, or external broadcasts. Begin Town Hall transition planning now. Run a pilot Town Hall with willing clients to test the experience before migrations are required.
LOW Meeting Notes for Instant Meetings (Loop-Powered)
End UsersRolling out: April 2026
Teams will introduce meeting notes for instant meetings (Meet Now, from chat), powered by Microsoft Loop. Participants can create agendas, capture notes, and assign action items during ad-hoc calls. Auto-enabled — no admin action needed.
Communicate to clients who do a lot of informal calls — this is a genuine productivity win. No admin prep needed, but proactive communication builds goodwill.
LOW Voice & Face Enrollment Dashboard for Admins
MSP AdminRoadmap 506750Rolling out now
Teams Admin Center now includes a dashboard showing voice and facial profile enrollment metrics for clients using AI-enhanced meeting features like Intelligent Recap. Provides enrollment trends over time.
Review in TAC for clients using Teams Rooms or Copilot Intelligent Recap. Useful for verifying AI meeting features are properly adopted.
LOW — VDI Clients Only Teams VDI — WebRTC Retirement, Move to SlimCore
MSP AdminMC1239928VDI Clients Only
Teams VDI is retiring WebRTC-based media optimization and transitioning to SlimCore-based optimization for Windows endpoints. Only affects clients running Teams in Citrix, AVD, or similar VDI environments.
Relevant only to VDI clients. Verify SlimCore compatibility in those environments now before WebRTC is removed.
