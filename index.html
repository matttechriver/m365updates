⚠️ Action Required / Deadlines 5 items
Semi-Annual Enterprise Channel Retirement — Unmanaged Devices
High Admin
⏰ April 6–11, 2026 — THIS WEEK
Microsoft is removing the Semi-Annual Enterprise Channel option for unmanaged devices in the M365 Apps admin center. Once removed, if an admin changes a device's channel, they cannot reselect Semi-Annual. Existing devices on this channel are not impacted.
Audit all client tenants using Semi-Annual Enterprise Channel for unmanaged devices before April 11. Lock in any desired settings now — the option disappears after this window.
SharePoint/OneDrive IDCRL Legacy Auth — Hard Deadline
High Both
⏰ May 1, 2026 — Extension expires April 30
IDCRL legacy SharePoint/OneDrive authentication is permanently retired May 1. After this date it cannot be re-enabled. Anything using stored username/password credentials — PowerShell scripts, Power BI refreshes, Power Automate flows, third-party tools — will break silently.
Sweep all client tenants for IDCRL-dependent scripts, flows, and tools. Migrate to OAuth/modern auth now. Any client using the temporary PowerShell extension has until April 30.
M365 Pricing Increases — July 1, 2026
High MSP
📅 July 1, 2026 — Communicate Now
Business Basic: $6→$7 (+16.7%) · Business Standard: $12.50→$14 (+12%) · Office 365 E3: $23→$26 (+13%) · M365 E3: $36→$39 (+8.3%) · M365 E5: $57→$60 (+5.3%)

Business Premium: Unchanged at $22/user/mo ✓
Build per-client pricing impact summaries. Business Premium's unchanged price makes the Standard→Premium upsell conversation compelling now. Clients renewing before July 1 lock in current pricing.
Teams Web Browser Enforcement
High Both
⏰ May 15, 2026 — Access blocked after this date
Teams on web will require ECMAScript 2022 (ES2022) browser support. Users on unsupported/old browsers will see warning banners until May 14, then lose access on May 15. Desktop and mobile clients are unaffected.
Identify clients with locked-down browser environments or legacy browser GPOs. Communicate to end users proactively — this will generate helpdesk tickets if caught off guard.
SharePoint OTP External Sharing → Entra B2B
High Both
📅 July 2026 — Plan Now
SharePoint One-Time Passcode (OTP) authentication for external sharing is being retired. External users without a corresponding Entra B2B guest account will get "Access Denied." Existing "specific people" links may fail for users without guest accounts.
Audit external sharing configurations across all client tenants. Identify active OTP-based vendor/partner sharing. Start Entra B2B guest account migration — this is a P1 incident waiting to happen if ignored.
🛡️ Security & Compliance 5 items
Purview Endpoint DLP — Always-On Diagnostics Opt-Out Window Closing
High Admin
⏰ Second week of April 2026 — opt-out window closes
Always-on diagnostics for Endpoint DLP will be enabled by default this week. Data includes policy evaluations, file classifications, enforcement actions — stored locally for 90 days. After the window, admins can still opt out at any time, but the default will be on.
Decide now per client tenant whether to opt out or accept the default. For most Business Premium clients with active DLP, leave it on — improves troubleshooting. Document the decision in Hudu.
Teams Suspicious Message Reporting Expanded to Defender P1
Medium Both
📅 Early–Late April 2026
Users can now report suspicious Teams messages (phishing, malware, spam) from chats, channels, and meeting chats. Previously required Defender P2 — now extended to Defender for Office 365 Plan 1, which is included in Business Premium.
Validate availability in Defender portal under Protection Reports → User-reported security submissions once rolled out. Include in security QBR talking points for Business Premium clients.
Teams External Domain Anomalies Report — New in TAC
Medium Admin
📅 Mid–Late April 2026
A new report in Teams Admin Center under Protection Reports shows cross-tenant communication patterns and flags sudden spikes or abnormal external engagement activity. Supports proactive BEC investigation and external collaboration oversight.
Add to routine TAC security review checklist. Integrate anomaly alerts into SIEM correlation workflows for elevated-risk clients.
Defender for iOS — iOS 16 End of Support
Medium Both
⏰ April 30, 2026
Microsoft Defender for iOS drops support for iOS 16 devices on April 30. These devices will no longer receive Defender updates, security patches, or support. New Defender for iOS installations also blocked on iOS 16 after this date.
Pull iOS device version reports from Intune/Ninja across client tenants. Flag iOS 16 devices for upgrade to iOS 17+ or hardware replacement before coverage gap opens April 30.
Cross-Tenant Entra Security Group Sync — GA Late April
Low Admin
📅 Late April 2026
Cross-tenant synchronization in Microsoft Entra now supports security groups in addition to users. Enables centralized group management and cross-tenant access. Requires admin opt-in with configured attribute mappings and access policies.
Low immediate impact for most SMB clients. Note for multi-entity clients or shared-services scenarios. Revisit during next licensing or identity architecture review.

Standalone SharePoint / OneDrive Plans — End of Sale Approaching
Medium MSP
📅 End of sale: May 31, 2026
Standalone SharePoint Online and OneDrive for Business (Plan 1 and Plan 2) are being retired. No new purchases after May 31, 2026. Existing customers can renew until end-of-life in January 2027, with full service through December 2029.
Check for any clients on standalone SPO/ODB plans — rare in your Business Premium-heavy base. Stop quoting these for new engagements immediately. Steer toward M365 suites for any new requirements.
